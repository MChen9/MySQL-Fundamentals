## Data Type

1. var OR varchar
	- var is fixed length, even input is not that long, and will truncate if too long, fast when fixed length
	- varchar is flexible
	
2. int OR decimal(len, d)
	- int is full number
	- decimal(len of total digits, 
	digits after decimal)
	
3. date OR time OR datetime
	- date: 'YYYY-MM-DD'
	- time: 'HH:MM:SS'
	- datetime: 'YYYY-MM-DD'
